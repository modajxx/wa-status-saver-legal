Privacy Policy

WA Status Saver  •  Effective date: 29 May 2026  •  Last updated: 29 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WA Status Saver ("we", "the app") handles your information when you use the app on your Android device. We built this app to do one thing — help you save WhatsApp statuses — without harvesting your data. The short version: we don't collect personal information, your statuses never leave your phone, and we don't use any third-party tracking.

1. What data the app collects

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email, phone number, contact list, or any account information
  • The photos, videos, or status content you view or save
  • Your location, IP address, or device identifiers
  • Your usage patterns or analytics events

We do not have a server. You do not create an account. Nothing leaves your phone.

2. Permissions the app requests

The app asks for the minimum permissions needed to function:

  • SAF folder access You manually pick the .Statuses folder once when you first launch the app. This is Android's Storage Access Framework — we receive permission only for the folder you select, and only for the URI you grant. You can revoke it at any time from the app's Settings or your phone's app settings.
  • Gallery write Used solely to copy the status into a "WA Status Saver" album in your phone gallery when you tap the download button. On Android 10+ (API 29+) this uses scoped MediaStore and does not require a runtime permission.

That's it. The app does not request Internet, Camera, Microphone, Location, or any other permission.

3. Third-party services

This version of the app does not integrate any third-party SDK, analytics, advertising, or tracking service. The app is fully self-contained and works offline.

4. Children's privacy

The app is rated for general audiences and does not knowingly collect data from anyone — adults or children.

5. Data sharing & selling

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data with third parties — because we do not collect any data to share.

6. Your rights

You always have the right to:

  • Revoke the folder access permission from the app's Settings → Folder access → Revoke
  • Delete the app at any time, which removes any locally cached data

7. Relationship with WhatsApp

WA Status Saver is an independent third-party app. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by WhatsApp Inc. or Meta Platforms, Inc. "WhatsApp" is a trademark of WhatsApp Inc. The app reads only the public .Statuses folder that WhatsApp writes to on your device. We do not use any private WhatsApp API or extract conversations.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via the app's release notes on Play Store. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page always reflects the current version. A future version of the app may introduce ads — if and when that happens, this policy will be updated to disclose what those ads collect, and you will be notified in the release notes.
